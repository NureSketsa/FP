How LearnVid AI Works

Create high-quality educational videos in 6 simple steps

  1. 1

    Sign Up & Log In

    Create your free account and log in to the chat dashboard.

  2. 2

    Start a New Chat

    Chat with the AI to ask questions or kick off a new video project.

  3. 3

    Turn On Video Mode

    Click the video icon beside the input box. When it lights up, Video Mode is on.

  4. 4

    Write Your Prompt

    Give a clear prompt. Example: "Create a video about integral calculus."

  5. 5

    Let It Generate

    The AI will process your request, write the script, and assemble the video. This can take a moment.

  6. 6

    Review & Download

    Your video appears right in the chat. Watch it and download it using the "Download Video" button.
